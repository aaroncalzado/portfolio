Title: Svergognato
----
Subtitle: In the summer of 2012 I had the opportunity to study abroad in Rome. During the five week seminar I spent most of my days observing the culture, art, architecture, and design of Italy. For my final project I designed, printed, and bound a one-off book highlighting my experiences in Italy.
----
Caption: Summer 2012, 5 weeks

Text: The word “svergognato” is an Italian word that roughly translates to “shameless” in English.
